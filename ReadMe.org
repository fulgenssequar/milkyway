** rules.js:

   The transforming rules for English postfixes:
   
*** -s
    meets -> meet
    
*** -g-n-i+e
    voting -> vote

*** -s-e-i+y
    treaties -> treaty
    
*** -g-n-i-%2-%s-%1$$
   sitting -> sit
   
- %1 = aeiou
- %2 = bcdfghjklmnpqrstvwxyz
- %s = same charactor
- $ = pop
